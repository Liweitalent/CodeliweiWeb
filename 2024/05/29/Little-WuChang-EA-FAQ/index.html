Little WuChang EA FAQ

中文

玩家你好，这里是“小小无常”抢先体验产品的常见问题页面。

早期访问包括哪些内容？
包含完整的主线流程以及所有故事结局。

游戏的当前状态是什么？
可以正常游玩，但是包含我们没有测试到的未知bug。
部分细节不够完美。

游戏将在Early Access中持续多久？
初步估计是2个月，我们会用这段时间来让正式版更加全面。

你多久更新一次游戏？
尽量保持在一周一更新。如果遇到卡住流程的恶性bug也会加快更新频率。

我的进度会在更新过程中保存吗？
是的，在不发生恶性bug的情况下，我们不会删除已有道具和进度内容，只会增加新的内容让流程完整。
而且我们的存档设计是支持兼容旧存档的。

当游戏离开Early Access时，价格会改变吗？
有可能，正式的版本价格会更高。

我可以提供反馈和错误报告吗？
是的，这对我们非常重要，可以通过邮件:921629263@qq.com 联系我们

In English

Hello player, this is the common question page for “Little WuChang” to experience the product first.

What is included in Early Access?
Includes the complete main storyline process and all story endings.

What is the current state of the game?
It can be played normally, but it contains unknown bugs that we have not tested.
Some details are not perfect enough.

How long will the game be in Early Access?
The initial estimate is 2 months, and we will use this time to make the official version more comprehensive.

How often will you be updating the game?
Try to keep updates on a weekly basis. If encountering malicious bugs that block the process, it will also accelerate the update frequency.

Will my progress be saved during updates?
Yes, without the occurrence of malicious bugs, we will not delete existing props and progress content, but only add new content to make the process complete.
And our archive design supports compatibility with old archives.

Will the price change when the game leaves Early Access?
It is possible that the official version will have a higher price.

Can I provide feedback and bug reports?
Yes, this is very important for us, and we can send it via email: 921629263@qq.com Contact us
